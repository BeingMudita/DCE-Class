Eggless Brownie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ cup yogurt (plain curd)
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a square baking pan and line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, mix yogurt, milk, sugar, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter).
  8. Let it cool completely before cutting into squares.
