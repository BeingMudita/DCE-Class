Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g melted unsalted butter
  • 400g cream cheese (softened)
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 150g powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200g fresh strawberries (for topping)
  • 2 tbsp strawberry jam or puree
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter.
  2. Press the mixture into the base of a springform pan and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  4. Add powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and lemon juice, mixing until combined.
  5. Whip the heavy cream separately until soft peaks form, then gently fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  6. Pour the filling over the chilled crust, smoothing the top.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight until set.
  8. Before serving, spread strawberry jam or puree on top and garnish with fresh strawberries.
  9. Slice and enjoy your creamy, eggless strawberry cheesecake!
