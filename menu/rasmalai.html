Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk (for chenna)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar (diluted in 2 tbsp water)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 liter full-fat milk (for rabri)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for rabri)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 saffron strands (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped pistachios
  • 2 tbsp chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. Prepare Chenna (Paneer): Boil 1 liter of milk. Add diluted lemon juice/vinegar gradually, stirring until the milk curdles. Strain using a muslin cloth and rinse with cold water to remove the sourness.
  2. Hang the cloth for 30 minutes to drain excess water. Knead the chenna until smooth and soft.
  3. Divide into small equal balls and flatten them slightly.
  4. Cook Rasmalai Patties: In a wide pan, boil 4 cups water and 1 cup sugar. Add chenna discs and cook for 12-15 minutes. They will double in size. Remove and let them cool.
  5. Prepare Rabri: Boil 1 liter of milk in a heavy pan. Simmer until it reduces to about 2/3 of its original volume. Add 1/2 cup sugar, saffron strands, and cardamom powder. Mix well.
  6. Squeeze the extra syrup from the cooked chenna patties gently and add them to the warm rabri.
  7. Garnish with chopped pistachios and almonds.
  8. Refrigerate for 3-4 hours before serving for best taste.
