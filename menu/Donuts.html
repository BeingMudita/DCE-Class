Eggless Donuts

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 cup warm milk (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Powdered sugar or melted chocolate for coating

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, powdered sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add melted butter, vanilla extract, and warm milk gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thickness and cut donut shapes using a cutter or two different-sized lids.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the donuts on medium heat until golden brown.
  6. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Coat the donuts with powdered sugar or dip in melted chocolate before serving.
