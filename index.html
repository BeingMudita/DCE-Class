Cheesecake is a popular dessert known for its rich, creamy texture and sweet, tangy flavor. It typically consists of a cheese-based filling (often cream cheese, but sometimes ricotta or cottage cheese) and a crust made from crushed cookies or graham crackers.

Donuts are a type of fried dough, typically ring-shaped with a hole in the center, and often enjoyed as a sweet treat. They come in various forms, including glazed, filled, and twisted, and are a popular choice for breakfast or snacks.

A brownie is a small, square-shaped chocolate baked good, often described as a cross between a cake and a cookie. They are known for their dense, fudgy or cakey texture and rich chocolate flavor. Brownies can be plain or contain additions like nuts, chocolate chips, or frosting.

Ras malai, also known as rasamalei, or roshmalai, is a dessert that originated in the Bengal region of the Indian subcontinent. The dessert is called roshmalai in Bengali, ras malai in Hindi, and rasa malei in Odia. It is popular in India, Bangladesh and Pakistan.
