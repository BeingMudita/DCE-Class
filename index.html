Bread pakora is a popular Indian street food and snack, consisting of bread slices dipped in a spiced gram flour batter and deep-fried until golden and crispy. It's a savory, deep-fried bread fritter, often stuffed with a spiced potato mixture. It's commonly enjoyed with chutney or ketchup, especially during the rainy season with a cup of chai.

Paneer tikka is a popular Indian dish consisting of marinated paneer (Indian cheese) cubes, typically grilled or baked, often in a tandoor (clay oven). It's a vegetarian alternative to chicken tikka and a common appetizer or snack. The dish is known for its smoky flavor, soft and juicy paneer, and vibrant spices.

A brownie is a small, square-shaped chocolate baked good, often described as a cross between a cake and a cookie. They are known for their dense, fudgy or cakey texture and rich chocolate flavor. Brownies can be plain or contain additions like nuts, chocolate chips, or frosting.
