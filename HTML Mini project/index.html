Bread Pakoda

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix the mashed potatoes with cumin seeds, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Spread the potato mixture between two slices of bread to make a sandwich.
  3. Cut the sandwich into triangles.
  4. Dip each triangle in a batter made from gram flour and water.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and fry the triangles until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes, bell pepper, and onion to the marinade. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  4. Grill on a barbecue or in an oven until the paneer is golden and slightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.
